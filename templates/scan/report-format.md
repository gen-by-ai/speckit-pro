# Contract — Merged Report format (`.knowledge/scan/latest.md`)

Reuses existing artifact shapes (R7). The Coverage Ledger is mandatory (FR-010).

```markdown
# Scan Report — <repo name>

> run_id: <id> | substrate: <in-harness|cli|sequential> | workers: <eff>/<req>
> portions: <leaf count> | wall-clock: <Ts> | serial baseline: <Ts> | speedup: <Nx>
> Generated by /pro.scan — claims carry provenance; verify before acting.

## Architecture Map
<!-- aligned with templates/knowledge/architecture.md -->
- <subsystem> — <one-line role> [evidence: path:line]

## Module / Dependency Overview
- <module> → depends on <module(s)> [evidence]

## Risk Hotspots
<!-- aligned with repo-map.prompt.md "Risks" -->
- <risk> — severity <high|med|low> [evidence]

## Conflicts
<!-- one block per merge-time disagreement; verdict + dissent both shown (Q4/FR-004) -->
- target: <x>
  - verdict (tie-breaker): <claim> [evidence]
  - dissent: <claim> [evidence]

## Unknowns
- <question the scan could not resolve>

## Coverage Ledger   <!-- MANDATORY — no silent gaps (FR-010, FR-018) -->
| portion | files | status | reason |
|---|---|---|---|
| p01 | 12 | analyzed | — |
| p07 | 3  | summarized | exceeded worker budget |
| p09 | 5  | failed | worker timeout (300s) |
```

Rules: every leaf portion appears in the Coverage Ledger exactly once; any `status != analyzed` requires a non-empty `reason`; if the engine capped coverage (top-N/sampling), a `> NOTE: capped …` line is added under the header.
